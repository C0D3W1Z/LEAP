Welcome to LEAP! LEAP is a small group of US based high school volunteers who believe in equal opportunity. We don't want students held back in any way due to a lack of English fluency, as it is a language which is used in almost all international interactions. Our goal is to provide a positive learning environment where each student feels appreciated. Thank you for any kind of involvement with our program.

Why Choose Us

Quality Education

We provide high-quality English language education to students.

At LEAP, we are committed to offering a top-notch education that focuses on language proficiency and communication skills. Our dedicated team of educators strives to create a nurturing environment where students can learn and grow.

Empowering Futures

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge they need for a brighter future.

We believe in the power of education to transform lives. By providing language education to these students, we aim to open doors of opportunity and give them the confidence to pursue their dreams.

Culturally Inclusive

We celebrate diversity and foster a culturally inclusive learning environment.

Our team values diversity and understands the importance of respecting different cultures. We embrace inclusivity and strive to create an environment where all students feel welcome and appreciated.

Our Services

English Language Classes

We offer interactive and engaging English language classes for students of all ages and skill levels.

Personalized Learning

Our educators tailor the curriculum to suit the individual learning needs of each student.

Progress Tracking

We monitor the progress of our students and provide regular feedback to parents and guardians.
